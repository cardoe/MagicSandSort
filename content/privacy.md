_Last updated: July 7, 2026_

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how **Magic Sand Sort**
("the app") handles your information. The short version: **we don't collect
any of it.**

## We do not collect personal data

The app does **not** collect, store, or share any personal information. In
particular:

- **No accounts.** You don't sign up or log in to play.
- **No tracking or analytics.** We don't use analytics tools or advertising
  identifiers, and we don't track your activity.
- **No third-party SDKs or ads.** The app contains no advertising and no
  third-party data-collection services.
- **Nothing leaves your device.** Any game progress is saved only on your
  device. It is not transmitted to us or to anyone else.

## Children's privacy

Because the app collects no personal information from anyone, it collects none
from children. The app is safe for players of all ages and does not knowingly
gather data from children under 13.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, we'll update this page and revise the "Last
updated" date above.

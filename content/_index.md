Pour colored sand from bottle to bottle until each bottle holds a single color.
Simple to learn and relaxing to play, **Magic Sand Sort** starts easy and builds
to tricky hard levels with limited turns — great for a quick brain-teaser or a
long, satisfying sorting session.

- 🧪 **Dozens of levels** that get more challenging as you go
- 🎯 **Turn-limited puzzles** and special hard levels to test your skills
- 🎨 **Colorful, playful design** with fun themes to unlock
- 👨‍👩‍👧 **Family-friendly** — no ads, no accounts, no data collected

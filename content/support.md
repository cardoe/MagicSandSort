Need help with **Magic Sand Sort**? We're happy to help.

## Contact us

Email us at [magicsandsort@thegoldsteins.org](mailto:magicsandsort@thegoldsteins.org)
and we'll get back to you as soon as we can.

## What to include

To help us solve your problem faster, please include:

- Your device model (for example, iPhone 15 or iPad Air)
- Your iOS/iPadOS version (Settings → General → About → Software Version)
- A short description of what happened, and what you expected to happen
- If possible, a screenshot showing the problem

## Frequently asked questions

**Does the game cost anything or have ads?**
NO

**Does the game work offline?**
YES

**How do I reset my progress?**

From the Start page, go to Settings, click Restart Progress.
